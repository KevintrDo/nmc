Thailand

FOOD: AMAZING
HIKING: AMAZING
NEAR DEATH EXPERIENCE: Sliped on rock, bang head ow.

Overall, the people living there are one of the nicest individuals I'll ever get to meet. Food is probably the best I've ever ate.
SEASONED TO THE MAX. But it brought me and others alike on a journey that I'll never forget.

Germany

FOOD: ITS ALRIGHT
HIKING: AMAZING
NEAR DEATH EXPERIENCE: ALMOST GOT LOST IN FOREST.

Lived there for two years. Wish I could live there forever. Food is so unseasoned unless the manager was Korean, American, or non-German.
Almost got lost in a black forest. Nearly died because is was 5PM!!! AND IT WAS ALMOST PITCH DARK. Overall, had some of my fondest memories made in Germany.

Australia

FOOD: ITS ALRIGHT
HIKING: ALMOST THE BEST
NEAR DEATH EXPERIENCE: BUSH CLIMBED 3000M MOUNTAIN THAT LASTED 10 HOURS (8am - 6pm).

Australia has one of the most vicious animals that will try to literally kill you. When I bushed climbed a mountain, I came across... Spiders, Leeches, centipedes, Boars, Birds, A FREAKING TASMANIAN DEVIL (ON A MOUNTAIN!!), and more.
Food was pretty rad, but lacked diversity. Went scuba diving at the grand barrier reef and sailed a boat. Also ziped lined and went cave diving where I also almost went into the deep dark because my instructors failed to catch me during a jump.
Overall, the best experience for adrenaline!

New Zealand

FOOD: SOOOO GOOD, TASTE REAL
HIKING: THE BEST
NEAR DEATH EXPERIENCE: NONE CAUSE ITS SOO PEACEFUL.

Went to a geological spring and mann that was good. Hiking had the best scenery known to mankind. Green all around with wildlife that will capture your eyes.
People are super nice and food taste real that I might think I'm living in a simulation.
Overall, will visit again for a 3 day hiking.
